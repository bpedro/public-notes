Most managers look at requests per second or latency to judge success. These are engineering metrics, not product metrics. They tell you if the system is running, but not if it is providing any real value to the business.

I have spent years looking at how APIs impact the bottom line. I’d focus on measuring time-to-first-call and retention rates to see if the product is actually useful.
