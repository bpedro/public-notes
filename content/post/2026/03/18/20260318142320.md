When every team uses a different style or format, the developer experience suffers. Users have to relearn how to interact with your company every time they try a new service. This inconsistency makes your platform feel disjointed and unprofessional.

I have led API standardization efforts across large organizations. I’d focus on creating shared patterns that allow developers to move between services without friction.
