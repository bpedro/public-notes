Most teams treat user research as a phase that ends before development starts. In a large organization, user needs can change as soon as they build an integration. A static research report is just a snapshot of a past that no longer exists.

My years of interviewing users show that discovery must be continuous. I’d focus on talking to consumers on an ongoing basis to see how their needs evolve after they go live.
