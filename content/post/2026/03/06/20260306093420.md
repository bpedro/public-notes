Most teams design for a generic "developer" and wonder why adoption is low. A frontend engineer and a data scientist have completely different requirements and workflows. If you try to please everyone, you build a tool that works for no one.

My experience with persona discovery shows that specificity wins. I’d focus on the daily friction of one specific type of user before writing a single line of code.
